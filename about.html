ABOUT ME..

Collaborating within teams of cross-functional roles, is an important aspect of my work ethos.

Joseph Anthony Roberts
Hello, I’m a British Website developer with significant experience with classic front-end frameworks including Bootstraps, Foundation, Semantic UI, Pure, and UKit. My knowledge also coincides with structuring technologies such as HTML, CSS, JQuery Django, React, Angular, Ruby and Laravel. I am also a startup and I am in the early-stages of my development, and always offering to make useful contributions towards successful product launches in multiple industries and markets around the world. My idea is to build applications which run as a services and whosoever seeks and finds it useful may also deploy or manage such applications for open source development. My intentions are to create practical and innovative solutions in response to social culture and environmental differences. I reside in the leafy county of Worcestershire in the UK, for most of my life. During this time I earned an Higher National Diploma in Industrial design. I believe design and technology heavily influences the work that I do and so I like to reflect that in some way or another.
